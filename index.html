ABOUT ME

Do you love to question how things work?

I do. I've been doing it for as long as I can remember, growing up on modding, video games, and reading hackerzines. I was never able to accept that things just are without thinking why. My curiosity for how computers worked lead me to study Computer Engineering at Penn State where I kindled interests in Logic, Mathematics, Design, Technology, and Economics. Though it took time to realize, the earlier experiences of growing up have been teaching me valuable and difficult lessons about myself, people, friendships, and life. I still spend most of my time pondering things to satisfy curiousity, and don't intend on stopping.

Arjan Kohli


Lately, my interests have been in fractal mathematics, data science, financial valuation, portfolio theory, cyclical market theory, sustainable farming, and country living.

When I'm not working I'm an avid outdoorsman and a fan of homecooking meals. I'm currently living in North Carolina with my two cats.
